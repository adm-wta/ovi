OVI Hydration - FAQ

What is OVI Hydration?

OVI is hydration, pure and simple. It’s made from fruit infused water, sea salt and honey. It contains nothing but delicious, authentic ingredients to rehydrate and restore.

What does OVI mean?

The name OVI is a combination of the words "Oh!" and "Vie." "Oh" is what you exclaim as you experience the delicious flavor, and "Vie" is the French word for "life” Once you have experienced OVI's flavor of life - we'd love to know what OVI means to you!

Does OVI Hydration have any artificial additives?

OVI Hydration is free from any artificial colors, sweeteners, preservatives and flavors.

Which countries are OVI Hydration available other than the United States?

OVI was first launched in Australia in 2014 and then in New Zealand in 2015.
